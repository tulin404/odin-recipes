3-Ingredient Chicken and Waffle Bites

Description

These deliciously simple Chicken and Waffle Bites are the perfect fusion of comfort food and crowd-pleasing convenience. Each bite-sized skewer features a piece of crispy, golden-brown popcorn chicken perched on a warm, fluffy mini waffle, finished with a generous drizzle of rich maple syrup. The combination creates a delightful balance of textures and flavors—crunchy, tender, sweet, and savory—all in one mouthwatering morsel.

Ideal for brunch spreads, game day snacks, or party appetizers, these bites are quick to assemble and guaranteed to disappear fast. With only three ingredients, they pack big flavor with minimal effort, making them a go-to favorite for both busy home cooks and last-minute hosts. Serve them warm with extra syrup on the side for dipping and watch them fly off the tray.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.6 ounce) jug shake and pour pancake mix, such as Bisquick Shake N Pour®
  • 1 1/2 pounds frozen fully cooked lightly breaded chicken tenders, such as Just Bare®
  • Cooking spray
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup, or as needed for dipping

Steps

  1. Prepare chicken tenders according to package directions.
  2. Prepare pancake mix according to instructions. Pour mixture into a bowl.
  3. Meanwhile, heat up a waffle iron. Spray waffle iron with nonstick spray. Take 1 to 4 chicken tenders at a time, depending on the size of your waffle iron, and dip tenders halfway into pancake batter; place onto the waffle iron. Cook until golden brown.
  4. Remove from the waffle iron to a plate; continue cooking remaining chicken.
  5. Serve immediately with syrup for dipping.
